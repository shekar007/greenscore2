Smart CSV Processing

Our system automatically detects and extracts relevant columns from your CSV, Excel, or PDF files. Column names are flexible!

  • Material Name - Required (material, item, product, name)
  • Quantity - Required (qty, quantity, amount, count)
  • Price - Required (price today, current price, selling price, price)
  • Unit - Optional (unit, units, measurement, uom)
  • Brand - Optional (brand, manufacturer, make)
  • Condition - Optional (condition, state, quality)
  • Specifications - Optional (specs, specifications, details)
  • Photo - Optional (photo, image, picture, url)

Note: Missing columns will be filled with default values. Invalid rows will be reported for correction.
